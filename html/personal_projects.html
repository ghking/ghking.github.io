Hood

December 2016

https://github.com/ghking/Hood

Hood is simple heads up display I made for my bedroom that tells the time and weather. I hooked it up to Raspberry Pi Zero so it turns on whenever my lights are on. Future plans are to add more modules and voice commands with Google Home.

Node.js, Raspberry Pi, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Dark Sky API, Sketch

Just Be

January 2015

http://www.northeastern.edu

During my last year at Northeastern, a few fellow students and I worked with a group of professors on a research project to help teach mindfulness to people exhibiting repressive tendenciess. We designed and developed an app called Just Be which allows users to listen to daily meditations and read weekly lessons outlining aspects of mindfulnes. We also wrote a companion app for the Pebble watch which was used for displaying reminders and notifications.

Pebble Smartwatch, Swift, Python, Sketch
