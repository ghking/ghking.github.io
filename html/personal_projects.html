Hood

December 2016

https://github.com/ghking/Hood

Hood is basically simple heads up display I made for my bedroom that tells the time and weather. I hooked it up to Raspberry Pi Zero so it's on whenever my lights are on. Future plans are to add other modules and add voice commands with Google Home.

Raspberry Pi, Javascript, HTML, CSS, Dark Sky API, Sketch

Just Be

January 2015

http://www.northeastern.edu

During my last year at Northeastern, a group of fellow students and I designed and developed an app called Just Be which to be used as a research tool for a group of professors. Just Be allowed subjects to listen to daily meditations and read lessons outlining an aspect of mindfulness, and collected feedback using a Pebble.

Pebble Smartwatch, Swift, Python (Django), Sketch

How Much Snow

December 2014

http://www.howmuchsnow.co

How Much Snow was a quick project a friend and I decided to build one snowy afternoon. That year, Boston was getting hammered with snow. We made a simple, one-fuction web page where people could see exactly how much snow the current storm was going to drop.

Node.js, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Dark Sky API, Heroku, Sketch
