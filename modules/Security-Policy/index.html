Security Policy

We employ a number of security measures to help keep your data safe. Your information is protected by Transport Layer Security (TLS) in transit, and 256-bit Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) at rest.

Our infrastructure is built on Firebase using the Google Cloud Platform (GCP). Firebase adheres to industry standard security, privacy and compliance controls, including:

  • ISO/IEC 27001, 27017 and 27018
  • SOC 1/2/3
  • PCI DSS
  • CSA STAR

We use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all of our systems, and routinely update them with the latest and most secure software to defend against vulnerabilities.
